Hunter Markets FAQ

Common questions with uncommonly good answers

For shoppers

FAQ Dropdown Hunter Markets FAQ
Where is Hunter Markets located? ▼

Catch us at 10–12 Balcombe Rd, Mentone. That’s a (500m) hot girl walk from the station on several bus routes. Get directions here.

Can I bring my pup? ▼

Absolutely! We’re dog-friendly — bring those good boys and girls along.

What's the vibe? ▼

Fun, friendly, and fashion-obsessed. Our stallholders come from far and wide to share their preloved wares with hundreds of eager shoppers every weekend.

Is Thrifters Market accessible? ▼

Yes! Hunter Markets is proudly accessible, because we’re committed to creating a safe, inclusive, and fun thrifting environment for EVERYONE. Expect flat surfaces, zero stairs, accessible doorways and an accessible coffee window service area, a roomy, clean accessible toilet and QR codes at accessible heights. Plus, we’re located only 500m from Mentone Train Station and other public transport options. But, if you have any concerns or questions ahead of your visit, please DM us, so we can make our market as comfortable and accessible for you.

What are your operating times? ▼

Find us thrifting between 9am and 1pm, every Saturday and Sunday. Err, except during Victorian public holiday long weekends.

What's the best time(s) to come shop. ▼

Yes, you’ll catch us thrifting between 9am and 1pm, but when’s the best time for you to arrive, specifically? 7am - 8am: If you’re an early birdie, and don't mind waiting in line with a hot coffee, you'll be one of the first to be let in with your pick of our stallholder's best items. We welcome shoppers inside at 9am. 10:30am - 11:30am: For those with well-worn snooze buttons, enjoy the sleep-in and take your time hunting through all the racks with a less chaotic crowd. 12pm - 1pm: Bargain baddies, unite. This is your time to snap up the best deals of the day as stallholders tend to reduce their prices during their final hour.

What do I need to bring with me? ▼

Bring your bestie, a tote, and $2 for entry. Also, bring cash ‘cause there’s no ATM in the warehouse. Oh, and don’t forget your KeepCup. We have a cute coffee window on-site.

Wait, why do you charge an entry fee? ▼

A $2 entry fee helps us maintain the vibes, quality clothes racks, and things like free tampons in the toilets.

Who do I pay when I'm there? ▼

See something you LOVE? Get it settled and all paid up with the individual stallholder before moving onto the next.

Can I try things on? ▼

Peeop your future look in our on-site change rooms. Always, always ask the stallholder before trying on that cute new ‘fit, and plz, don’t use the toilets as change rooms. Plz.

Wait, where's the closest cafe? ▼

Read my perfectly glossed lips. Coffee. On. Site. Quench that thrifting thirst at our coffee window, outside the front of our warehouse. Catch @10balcombe, serving delicious @st_ali coffee – and the occasional snaccy too!

Where should I park? ▼

Whether you’re a driver or a passenger princess (beep, beep), please know that there’s limited street parking available on weekends.

Are all the items second hand? ▼

Step into our big purple warehouse and you’ll find preloved, vintage, thrifted or gifted (did someone say NWT?!) clothing, shoes and accessories.

What if I can't find anything in my size? ▼

We strive to cater to all bodies, sizes and fits by welcoming stallholders with the same values. So that our shoppers get a good mix, we never assume a seller’s size and actively seek to include a range of vendors in our accepted applications. So ultimately, this depends on who wants to sell with us. Want to hunt through a certain influencer’s wardrobe? Name drop them by emailing hello@huntermarkets.com.au

Do you offer returns? ▼

Unfortunately, no. Based on the nature of the market, all sales are final. Choose wisely and make sure to chat with the individual stallholder about the item you’re interested in before finalising payment.

What if I wanna work with you? ▼

Slide on over to our Work with us page for collabs, partnerships, promo and PR opportunities.
